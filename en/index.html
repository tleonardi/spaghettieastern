What happens to the money that you donate

Paypal donations to the team

All the donations received via Paypal (or directly to one of the team members) will be used to cover some of the expenses necessary to realise this project. For example, these donations will be used for:

  • The vehicle (which will be donated to charity once we arrive)
  • Spare parts for the vehicle (which are also being donated once we arrive)
  • Mongolian import taxes on the vehicle

We will NOT USE the donations to pay for:

  • Accommodation during the trip
  • Our return flight
  • Personal expenses during the trip

    • All the unspent money will be donated to GoHelp and Actionaid

    Donation to Go Help via Justgiving

    All the donations that we receive via JustGiving are entirely for charity and go directly to GoHelp.
